Questions, answered

Who can invest?

At launch, InvestHome is available to non‑resident individuals in select countries. Join the waitlist to be notified as we expand.

What currencies do you support?

USD, GBP, and EUR for funding. Returns can be held in USD or converted based on your preference and availability.

Are returns guaranteed?

No. All investments carry risk, including loss of capital. We provide transparent data and risk disclosures for informed decisions.

How are fees structured?

Simple and transparent: a platform fee per deal and, where applicable, a performance fee aligned to net returns. Full breakdown per opportunity.
